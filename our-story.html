Our Story

At La Pizzeria, we don’t just make pizza. We make people happy. La Pizzeria was built on the belief that pizza night should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do. With more than 3+ years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles: We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.

La Pizzeria is largest online Pizza Ordering and Delivery platform, founded in February 2019. La Pizzeria is based in New York, United States and as of August 2022, is operating 700+ outlets in World Wide.

We’re not for people who want to blend in: pushing boundaries is part of our heritage. We have more than 700+ restaurants and 32,000 team members in more than 50 countries. Whether it’s the original Stuffed Crust or putting a pizza in outer space, we never stop driving ourselves to deliver hot pizzas, fast every time – anywhere you want to enjoy it.

The last thing the world needed was another pizzeria, but maybe this one could be different and everyone could get exactly what they wanted, made fresh on demand.
